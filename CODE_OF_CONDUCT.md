# Code of Conduct

*← [[README|README]] · [[00_MOC|Orchestration OS]].*

This project follows the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

Examples of behavior that helps: empathy and kindness, respect for differing opinions, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, public or private harassment, and publishing others' private information without permission.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate.
